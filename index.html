Why

US businesses spend $160 Billion per year in training and developing their management and employees
Industry experts estimate that 75% ($120 Billion) of that training is lost and not put into use, because there is not specific reinforcement and coaching to implement the new training. Current post training coaching solutions are costly, non-scalable, often generic and not tied directly to the management training and development principles and elements. Additionally, corporate learning and development leaders have limited visibility of what training elements are being implemented and what the impact is on their organization performance.

The solution to broad implementation of learning & development agendas is to implement customized, mobile AI coaching capability built on the principles and curriculum of the training.

AI Coach™ aligns its machine learning capabilities to each learning and development agenda, leading to greater implementation of new training principles.

I Coach™ bridges the gap of cost-effective scalability across broad management populations. This customization enhances the management training efforts resulting in greater use of “lessons learned”. AI Coach is the only custom, mobile AI coaching platform that utilizes specific training principles from any vendor to improve retention, utilization and ROI. AI Coach™ is the first customizable, mobile platform that will provide insightful coaching for managers following their specific training and development investment.
